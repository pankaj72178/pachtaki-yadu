About Our Village

Bairgania (Pachtaki Yadu) is a vibrant rural village in Bihar, dedicated to sustainable development and community welfare. Our village represents the true spirit of Indian democracy and grassroots governance.

📊 Village Statistics

  • 👥 Population: ~5,000+
  • 🏘️ Total Wards: 5
  • 🌾 Primary Occupation: Agriculture
  • 📍 District: North Bihar
